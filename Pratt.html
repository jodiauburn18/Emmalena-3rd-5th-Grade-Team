Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Brandie Pratt, and I am the 3rd-5th grade Special Education Teacher at Emmalena Elementary!


I have taught for 5 years, and this will be my fourth year at Emmalena Elementary.


I earned my bachelor's degree in psychology at Midway University. Additionally, I earned my master's degree in special education at the University of the Cumberlands.


If you have any questions, you can message me on class dojo or email me at brandie.pratt@knott.kyschools.us!

Class Rules

1. Listening Bodies- I will listen and follow directions.

2.Raised Hands- I will raise my hands to share ideas.

3.Quiet Mouths- I will use a soft mouth.

4.Walking Feet- I will walk in school to stay safe.

5.Helping Hands- I will use my hands for helping and not hurting.

6.Caring Hearts- I will use kind words.

Weekly Homework

Each week, I send home ways to practice for each of my students' individual yearly goals. Nothing is "due" except a parent/guardian signature each week, indicating that the student did complete their practice.

Curious About Special Education?

If I already service your child, please be on the lookout for their progress updates! I will send them home in an envelope in their folder!

If you are interested in learning more about the special education process or if you have questions, please feel free to reach out to me by calling (606) 251-3651 or emailing me at brandie.pratt@knott.kyschools.us. I would love to provide you with more information!
