Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Kasey Turner, and I am the 3rd-5th grade Reading & ELA Teacher at Emmalena Elementary!


I have taught for one year, and this will be my first year at Emmalena Elementary.


I earned my bachelor's degree in elementary education at Alice Lloyd College. One of my favorite hobbies is to run! I am currently training for my first marathon!


If you have any questions, you can message me on class dojo or email me at kasey.turner@knott.kyschools.us!

Class Rules

1. Listen when others are talking.

2.Raise your hand for permission to speak.

3.Follow directions.

4.Keep your hands, feet, and objects to yourself!

5.Work quietly and do not disturb others!

Weekly Focus

3rd-
RL.3.1 Ask and answer questions, and make and support logical inferences to construct meaning from the text.

4th-
RI.4.2 Analyze how the central ideas are reflected in a text, and cite relevant implicit and explicit evidence from the text.

5th-
RI.5.1 Quote accurately from a text when explaining what the text says explicitly and when drawing inferences from the text.
